Executive Summary
Audio Superb CC proposes a phased collaboration with Proking Solutions to develop a
vehicle-centric digital product catalogue and a vehicle-based search/AR engine. The project has two
objectives: to produce a structured catalogue with a standard four-image visual set per vehicle, and
to implement a website engine that allows customers to retrieve these visuals by searching for their
vehicle make, model, and year. The work will be delivered across three phases: dashboard image
scraping, catalogue image generation, and engine integration.1. Project Overview
Audio Superb CC is proposing a structured development project with Proking Solutions to create a
vehicle-based trim kit catalogue and a website engine that enables customers to search by
vehicle make, model, and year and retrieve accurate product imagery.
2. Project Objectives
Objective 1 – Catalogue: Create a digital catalogue displaying trim kits by compatible vehicle using a
standardised four-image visual set per vehicle.
Objective 2 – Engine: Develop a website engine or bot enabling customers to search by vehicle and
retrieve compatible trims and images.
3. Phased Project Structure
Phase 1 – Dashboard Image Scraping & Data Collection
Proking Solutions will scrape and source dashboard images for exact vehicle make, model, and
year. An initial vehicle list will be provided by Audio Superb. The estimated volume is approximately
200 dashboards, subject to refinement.
Phase 2 – Catalogue Image Generation
Audio Superb will provide trim-only images. Proking Solutions will generate, using AR and AI
techniques, the screen inserted into the trim and the screen plus trim installed into the dashboard,
leveraging both existing and generated assets to create a complete four-image catalogue entry per
vehicle.
Phase 3 – Website Engine / AR Integration
Proking Solutions will implement a vehicle-based search or bot on the Audio Superb website,
enabling customers to select their vehicle and view compatible trim kits and associated images.
4. Catalogue Image Standard
Each catalogue entry will display the following four images: trim only; screen inserted into
trim; vehicle dashboard; and trim plus screen installed into the dashboard.
